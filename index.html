Intro

Hello, I'm Punit Yadav, a professional trainer with 2 years of experience and having trained over 100 clients, including state-level athletes. I combine expert knowledge and diverse skills to create dynamic, personalized training programs. I focus on improving strength, flexibility, mental discipline, and self-defense techniques.

My training approach emphasizes both physical conditioning and mental focus, guiding clients through intense workouts, precise techniques, and mindfulness practices. Whether preparing athletes for competition, helping individuals enhance fitness, or introducing beginners to martial arts, I provide a safe, motivating environment that fosters personal growth and mastery of the craft.

About

Hello, I'm Punit Yadav, a professional trainer with a Master's in Sports Science and a state-level player with multiple match wins. With over 2 years of experience and having trained more than 100 clients, I offer personalized training as well as group sessions designed to enhance both physical and mental well-being. My training expertise spans boxing, yoga, Muay Thai, karate, and combat sports, and I focus on creating tailored regimens that improve strength, flexibility, mental discipline, and self-defense skills.
